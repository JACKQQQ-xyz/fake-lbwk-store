About liberty Walk

Liberty Walk (LBWK) is a Japanese automotive company founded by Wataru Kato, specializing in custom wide-body kits and modifications for high-performance cars like Ferrari, Lamborghini, and Nissan. Known for its bold and aggressive designs, LBWK has gained global recognition in the tuning community. The brand offers various product lines, including LB★Works, LB★Performance, and LB★Nation, catering to different styles and vehicle types. Beyond body kits, Liberty Walk also provides apparel, accessories, and scale models. With Liberty Walk USA as its official distributor, the company continues to push the boundaries of automotive customization while staying true to its Japanese street-racing heritage.
